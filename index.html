Columbia IL Volunteers is Updating To Monroe Co Volunteers

Columbia IL Volunteers is *transitioning to* Monroe Co*.* IL Volunteers. Don't worry, if your organization was a part of Columbia, IL Volunteers, your account will be transferred over.

Why The Change?

About 5 years ago, this site started as a 'student' project, and it's long long overdue for a major makeover! I hope to launch a newer, updated experience in early 2018.

With the update, we hope to encompass a larger area including both Columbia, Waterloo, Red Bud, etc. - all of Monroe County!

Meanwhile, do check back for any updates. For any questions, comments or concerns, or if you would like to help with building and/or testing the new upcoming site, please email: manavm@visionwebsoft.com. Thanks for your ongoing and continued support.

- Manav Misra
